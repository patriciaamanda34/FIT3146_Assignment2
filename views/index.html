Prambanan Temple

Prambanan, village in the daerah istimewa (special district) of Yogyakarta, Indonesia, known for a large, nearby complex of temples built in the 9th and 10th centuries. These temples were designated a UNESCO World Heritage site in 1999.


Nobody knows the origin of the Roro Jonggrang legend, which is also the Legend of Prambanan Temple, but it was possibly created when the locals discovered the temple. At that time, the locals had no means to understand the temple. Thus, the legend of Roro Jonggrang was formed.
